Addresses are meant to be free.

Address data is essential infrastructure. Street names, house numbers and zip codes, when combined with geographic coordinates, are the hub that connects digital to physical places.

OpenAddresses is open data released under a CC0 License. Individual datasets carry respective licence.

Parse or import them into a database, put on a map, or use them for geocoding. The choice is yours.

Download the collection .csv

Help contribute. Grow OpenAddresses by contributing data from any place in the world. Visit the contribution page to get started.
